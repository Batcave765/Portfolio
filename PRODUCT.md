# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Recruiters and hiring managers reviewing Sriram M.'s experience quickly, often between interviews or while comparing candidates. They need to understand his engineering focus, credible impact, and how to contact him without digging through a conventional résumé layout.

## Product Purpose

A portfolio that turns Sriram's résumé into a memorable, easy-to-scan case for his work in performance engineering, automation, and full-stack systems. Success is a visitor understanding his current role, strongest evidence, and project range before choosing to open the résumé or start a conversation.

## Positioning

An engineer who turns demanding systems into faster, more reliable tools and workflows.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: open the résumé.
- Secondary CTA: email Sriram.
- Remembered line: performance-minded engineering for systems people depend on.
- Belief ladder: Sriram has a clear engineering focus; he has applied it to production-scale work; he builds deeply across application layers; he is ready to talk.
- Proof on hand: detailed work history, quantified 3,000 TPS and 3–4 hour time-saving outcomes, two project case studies, awards, and education in `public/SWEResume.html`.

## Brand Personality

Signal-painted, precision-cut, kinetic. The experience should feel confident, bright in its energy even in dark mode, and unmistakably personal rather than corporate or terminal-themed.

## Anti-references

Avoid terminal cosplay, generic developer-dashboard UI, uniform boxed sections, card grids, gloomy black-on-gray dark mode, and default editorial-magazine styling.

## Design Principles

1. Make proof visible before biography.
2. Let typography and space carry the identity; the portrait supports it.
3. Give every section its own rhythm while preserving a coherent reading path.
4. Treat responsiveness and reduced motion as first-class composition constraints.
5. Keep interaction focused and content accessible without adding technical weight.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic structure, keyboard-reachable controls, 44px minimum touch targets, clear focus states, WCAG AA contrast targets, and a reduced-motion alternative for all nonessential animation.
